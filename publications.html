Multilevel Stein variational gradient descent

Stein variational gradient descent approximates a target distribution by iteratively updating an ensemble of particles. The performance of the method largely depends on how close the initial ensemble of particles is to the target distribution. Multilevel Stein variational gradient descent (MLSVGD) reduces the computational burden of SVGD by successively training with respect to cheaper surrogate models.

Multifidelity covariance estimation

Covariance estimation arises in many different applications: from dimensionality reduction and hypothesis testing in statistics, understanding model uncertainty in computational science, and financial applications. Typically, covariance estimates can have a high variance.
