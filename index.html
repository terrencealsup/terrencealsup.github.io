Bio:

I obtained my Ph.D. in mathematics at New York University working with Dr. Benjamin Peherstorfer. Before moving to New York City I graduated from Georgia Tech with a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics in 2016 and an M.Sc. in Mathematics in 2017.

My research is on the topic of multifidelity and multilevel methods for uncertainty quantification. More generally I'm interested in scientific computing, machine learning, and statistical inverse problems.

I also enjoy playing tennis, learning about wine, and hiking in the Hudson valley.
